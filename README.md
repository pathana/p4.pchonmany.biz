p4.pchonmany.biz
================

Project 4

Band Central is a website that connects the fans (users) to their favorite bands who are registered on the website. Bands update 
their fans with their latest news from the band themselves and the fans can follow these updates after registering on the site.

---

• Register/login to the site  
• View and edit user profile  
• View a list of all bands on site  
• Follow bands to get their latest new updates from the band themselves  
• Unfollow bands  
• Dashboard to view all posts from the bands you follow  

---

All forms in the site use JavaScript validation.
